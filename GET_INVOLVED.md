# Get Involved with Luminis Foundation

Thank you for your interest in Luminis Foundation.

Luminis Foundation is being built as a public-benefit initiative focused on sovereign AI, privacy-first systems, decentralized governance, regenerative technology, and human-centered intelligence.

## Ways to Get Involved

### Contribute Ideas
Share mission-aligned ideas, proposals, and suggestions that can strengthen the foundation’s direction, structure, research, or public presence.

### Help Build
Contribute to documentation, design, writing, strategy, research, development, or public-facing materials.

### Collaborate
Join as a mission-aligned builder, researcher, designer, writer, strategist, or supporter interested in helping shape the foundation.

### Improve the Public Repository
Review the README, roadmap, mission, and vision documents. Open issues or pull requests to suggest improvements, additions, or refinements.

## Who We Welcome
We welcome contributors who care about:
- Privacy and sovereignty
- Ethical and decentralized technology
- Regenerative and nature-aligned systems
- Human dignity and long-term flourishing
- Public-benefit innovation

## How to Participate
1. Read the core documents in this repository
2. Review the mission, vision, and roadmap
3. Open an issue for ideas, questions, or proposals
4. Submit a pull request for specific improvements

## Current Stage
Luminis Foundation is currently in its formation stage. This is an open collaboration hub for shaping the foundation’s direction, structure, and public materials.

## Contact and Collaboration
More formal collaboration pathways, outreach channels, and participation opportunities will be added as the foundation develops.
