# Luminis Foundation Roadmap

## Phase 1 - Foundation Formation
- Define mission, vision, and values
- Build the public website
- Establish the public collaboration hub
- Draft core documentation and strategy

## Phase 2 - Public-Benefit Infrastructure
- Expand foundation documentation
- Publish research directions
- Develop public educational materials
- Build early community contribution processes

## Phase 3 - Sovereign AI and Research
- Support the development of privacy-first and offline AI systems
- Explore decentralized governance frameworks
- Research memory-centered intelligence systems
- Investigate regenerative and nature-aligned technology concepts

## Phase 4 - Long-Term Ecosystem Development
- Expand the foundation into a broader public-benefit technology initiative
- Support open collaboration across research, design, and development
- Develop long-term programs around ethical AI, resilience, and ecological alignment

## Current Status
Luminis Foundation is currently in its formation stage.
